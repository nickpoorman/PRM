Engineering The future is now.

In the past, sailors often found transporting boats to be extremely painful. There are a great number of preparations required when transporting a boat by trailer, such as having the trailer inspected and registered, checking the brake lights, and also strapping everything down. Regattable was born in the minds of our engineers who have come up with a solution to this problem.

Traditional collapsible or portable sailboats utilize inflatable hulls. Although this alternative is compelling when considering portability, the boats do not perform like true sailboats. Using state-of-the art technology Regattable brings performance and portability into the sailing industry.

Design See for yourself.

Our design utilizes space age materials such as carbon fiber and nylon composites to bring to light a high performance sailboat that can be stored in a closet and transported by car or plane. Not only does our design allow for collapsibility, it provides ease of transport due to its low weight. With rigorous testing through software simulations, prototyping and re-iteration of design Regattable strives to bring forth a high-quality product worth sailing.

Performance Competitive racing.

During the inception of Regattable we gave performance a great deal of thought. We wanted to build a sailboat that was fun and enjoyable for families but could also be raced by competitive sailors. In the past, boats have been designed with a vast number of optional performance upgrades, making competitive racing extremely costly to sailors. By racing one-design or in the case of our boats, one-make racing (where all racers are required to have a stock boat), we are able to keep cost lower for anyone wishing to race competitively.
