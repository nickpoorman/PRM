Anthony DiMare
Founder

Anthony DiMare is a mechanical engineering student at Syracuse University. A true adrenaline junkie, DiMare is always seeking a thrill in favorite activities such as windsurfing, skiing, and cliff jumping. His long-held love of sailing goes back to his first job, in which he managed and maintained a fleet of sailboats in Massachusetts for 4 years. Having worked in product development for medical equipment, DiMare understands the importance of attention to detail; the basis behind the Regattable design.
